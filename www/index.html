Pancakes


Ingredients
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 3 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 Tblspn sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/8 tsp vanilla
  • 1 1/2 cups milk

Instructions
  1. mix all dry ingredients
  2. add egg and milk
  3. whisk gently until combined
  4. don't over mix, should be a bit lumpy
  5. let stand a few minutes, will become fluffy
  6. gently scoop on to hot griddle
  7. cook until edges are dry, flip once
